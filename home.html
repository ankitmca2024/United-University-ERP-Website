About Us - The United University, Prayagraj

United Group established in the year 1998 with its glorious journey of 27 years in the field of education; highly qualified and competent team of faculty; enormous world-class infrastructure & facilities most conducive to academic pursuit along with its remarkably successful campus placements, proudly shares the launch of its revolutionizing dream venture -

United University, approved by the State Government under the Private University Act!

UNITED UNIVERSITY is a newly emerging, multidisciplinary and one of the most promising Universities set up to facilitate quality research and training with the launch of its courses across the academic spectrum spanning Arts, Sciences, Commerce, Engineering, Management, Agriculture, Mass Communication etc.

UU is at the forefront of learning, teaching and research in various fields and envisages providing education par excellence and contributing towards empowering the nation by producing skilled professionals!

Vision, Mission & Core Values

* Vision:To established a Value based Global University having dynamic learning environment encouraging creativity and innovation, research inspired experimental learning and focusing on topics that are pertinent to the development of the region, the Country and the World.

* Mission:

  • To provide a dynamic, inspiring, and varied learning environment with global exposure.
  • To position the institution as a premier hub for research and experiential learning.
  • To develop into an adaptable university meeting the demands of society and business.
  • To incorporate Value thinking, integrity, wisdom and passion in professional for their career and life.
  • To foster a culture of continuous improvement and lifelong learning among students and faculty.

* Core Values:

In keeping with achieving the Vision and Mission of the University we commit ourselves to the following core values:

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in education, research, and community service.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and new ideas to solve real-world problems.
  • Inclusivity: Fostering a diverse and inclusive environment for all students and staff.
  • Collaboration: Building partnerships with industry, government, and communities to enhance learning and research.
